African Recipes

Africa has a wide range of delicous food but for this article we would focus on one.

Ewa Agoyin

  • Beans-500g
  • Short Chilli-200g
  • Stock cube
  • Crayfish
  • Fresh Pepper
  • Tatashe&Onions
  • Onions
  • Palm Oil

Cook your beans until its very soft and place in a colander when cooked. Put your dry pepper, fresh pepper, onions and tatashe and blend. Bleach your oil,add your blended pepper and fry your sauce until thoroughly cooked to a brownish color.
